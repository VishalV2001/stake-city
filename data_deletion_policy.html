User Data Deletion Instructions

If you would like to request the deletion of your personal data from the Stake City Project, please follow these instructions:

  1. Send an email to support@yourdomain.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request".
  2. Include your account information and any other relevant details in the email.
  3. We will process your request within 30 days and inform you once your data has been deleted.

Data We Collect:

We collect the following data from users:

  • Email address
  • Profile information
  • Any other data you choose to share

Contact Us:

If you have any questions about our data deletion policy, please contact us at support@yourdomain.com.
